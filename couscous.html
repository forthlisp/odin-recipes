Couscous, the quinoa alternative.

Couscous, a good alternative to quinoa.

To make a bowl of couscous, all you need are three things.

Follow the recipe and you should not fail!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Couscous
  • Water

STEPS:

  1. Pour water into a pot
  2. Put the water to boil
  3. Put couscous in a bowl
  4. Pour water over couscous
  5. After five minutes, lift the lid and fluff the couscous with a fork
  6. Enjoy your perfect bowl of couscous!
